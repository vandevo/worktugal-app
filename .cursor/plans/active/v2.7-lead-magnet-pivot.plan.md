# v2.7 Lead Magnet & Results Page Pivot

## Rationale

The 149 EUR clarity call CTA on the results page creates friction at the wrong moment. A user who just learned they have compliance risks needs to process that information, not immediately face a purchase decision. The trust-first model (established in v2.6) is most effective when the results page is pure value — no selling. The call offer goes into an email sequence where the user has already had time to absorb their results.

The Facebook group is dead. Showing it creates a false signal of community health. Telegram only.

## Decision: What moves where

| Element | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| 149 EUR clarity call CTA | On results page (prominent) | Email sequence only |
| Sticky bottom bar | Always visible with call CTA | Removed |
| Community section | Telegram + Facebook dual buttons | Telegram only |
| Freshness signal | Homepage badge only | Homepage + results page |

## Files changed

- `src/components/diagnostic/DiagnosticResults.tsx` — 3 targeted changes

## What does NOT change

- All trap data, scores, segment messages
- The email gate in `DiagnosticForm.tsx`
- `VITE_CLARITY_CALL_URL` env var (stays for email sequence use)
- The `parallel_ai_live_integration.plan.md`
- The rebuild plan's monetization ladder (clarity call is still Layer 1 — delivery channel changes, not the offer)

## When to restore the on-page CTA

After the email sequence is built and produces measurable bookings, consider adding the Cal.com link back as a lower-prominence secondary option below the Telegram CTA. Do not restore the sticky bar. Do not restore before email sequence is live.
