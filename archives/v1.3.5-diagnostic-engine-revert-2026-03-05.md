## 1. Thread Overview

• Scope, tech, funnels, ops, partners, events, finance:
The session focused on migrating the "Setup Check" from `setup.apps.worktugal.com` to `app.worktugal.com`, creating Diagnostic Engine v2. The implementation included a new database table `compliance_diagnostics` with `user_id` and dual scoring (Setup Score and Exposure Index).

• Emotional or strategic drivers, survival pressure, leverage:
The strategic driver was shifting from "Tax" terminology to "Compliance Intelligence" to reduce jargon and increase user trust through risk assessment. The goal was to build a modular system for multiple countries, starting with Portugal.

• Outcome summary and why it matters:
The implementation was completed but subsequently reverted at the user's request. The user was unhappy with the changes and the disruption to the existing "Readyfile v1.2" flow. The site was restored to its original state and verified through production builds.
