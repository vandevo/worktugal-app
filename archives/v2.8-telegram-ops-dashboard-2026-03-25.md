Both TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN and TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID were updated in Supabase secrets and synced to the local .env.

The session also clarified the two-bot architecture: @worktugal_bot (8112126887) handles the public @worktugal Telegram channel. @WorktugalPassBot (7495029105) handles all ops and diagnostic alerts to Van's private chat.
