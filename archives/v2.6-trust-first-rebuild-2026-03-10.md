# Thread Handover Protocol

## 1. Thread Overview

• Scope, tech, funnels, ops, partners, events, finance:
Transitioned the Worktugal diagnostic from a freemium content-gate to a "trust-first" lead generation engine. Removed the 29 EUR Stripe paywall for detailed results; all legal citations and penalty ranges are now free. Rebuilt the homepage (`/`) for mobile-first "doom scrollers," removing the fake search bar and replacing testimonials with concrete data metrics. Hidden the accountant application portal (`/accountants/apply`) to focus solely on generating clarity call demand first.

• Emotional or strategic drivers, survival pressure, leverage:
The founder (solopreneur) felt the free results were "half-ass" and wanted a sleeker, more professional product to distribute effectively. Recognizing a geopolitical tailwind (Middle East instability driving HNW expats to Portugal), the strategy shifted to build immense trust upfront. By giving away the "what" (the traps), we sell the "how" (the personalized action plan via a 149 EUR clarity call). The focus is on rapid deployment and revenue generation to maintain momentum.

• Outcome summary and why it matters:
Version 2.6 is fully shipped locally. The diagnostic now acts as a high-value lead magnet that doesn't hold back critical information. The homepage is optimized for a 5-7 second attention span, leading with hard numbers (penalties, completion counts). This positions Worktugal to immediately capture high-intent leads and drive them to immediate cashflow (clarity calls), deferring complex partner network building until demand is proven.
