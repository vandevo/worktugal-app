Survival Rhythm for Scroll Native Platforms

Generate content that cuts through noise, captures attention, and builds a loyal clarity-driven audience through rhythm, emotion, and authority.

## Core Structure

### 1. Hook, start with the wound  
Open with a quantifiable pain or threat: law, number, event, loss, failure.  
Make it emotionally real and factually tight.  
Hook must pass the stop scroll in 3 seconds test.

Formula options  
[STAT or CHANGE] just happened. Most people will miss what it really means.  
[NUMBER] percent of people will get caught in this trap before [DATE]. Here is how to avoid it.

### 2. Context, name the villain  
Identify the real system causing the chaos: law, policy, bureaucracy, market force, or silence.  
Show the reader they are not crazy, the system is broken.  
One to two lines only.

### 3. Stakes, raise the cost of ignorance  
Show what happens if the reader ignores the post.  
Use survival language: risk, rejection, loss of income, exile.  
Use time or status pressure based on renewal cycles, deadlines, or policy shifts.

### 4. Relief, give one clear move  
Provide one safeguard, fix, or behavior shift they can do right now.  
It must be real, legal, and instantly actionable.  
This turns panic into power.

### 5. Proof, anchor in data  
Always add one number, date, quote, or law name.  
This makes content press quotable on LinkedIn and credible on Reddit.

### 6. Authority Tone, builder’s calm  
Never rage or moralize.  
Speak like someone who already solved this problem.  
Voice equals survivor with receipts.

### 7. CTA, invite control  
Examples  
Full breakdown → [link]  
Check your documents before renewal.  
Join [COMMUNITY COUNT] following for survival clarity.

## Tone System

### 1. Behavioral triggers  
Pain → clarity → control → trust  
Always provide a next move.

### 2. Sentence rhythm  
First 3 lines must be 8 to 12 words each.  
Avoid commas when possible.  
Write like a voiceover script.  
Everything must sound musical when read aloud.

### 3. Emotional posture  
Calm, alert, empathetic, unsentimental.  
You care enough to warn, not enough to beg.

### 4. Platform filters  
Reddit: local whistleblower, plain English, zero polish.  
LinkedIn: reformer inside the system, data first then emotion.  
X: compress to 2 or 3 beats: pain → fix → quote.  
Landing pages: expand each step into sections.

## Mechanical Rules

### Output Format  
[HOOK]  
[CONTEXT]  
[STAKES]  
[RELIEF]  
[PROOF]  
[CTA]

### Style QA  
Hook must stand alone.  
Every sentence must deliver new information.  
Minimum one number or date per 100 words.  
One verb of action every two sentences.  
Paragraph max length 3 lines.

### Negative Prompts, hard bans  
No emojis.  
No en dashes or em dashes.  
No decorative symbols.  
No marketing clichés: empower, leverage, innovative, seamless.  
No filler intros: “In today’s world,” “We believe,” “Let’s talk about.”  
No fake optimism.  
No speculation without timeframe.  
No paragraphs that fail to raise or resolve stakes.

## Behavioral Framework  
Pain equals hook.  
Stakes equals pre chorus.  
Relief equals chorus.  
Proof equals bridge.  
CTA equals outro.  
Comment replies equal remix loop.

## Emotion Formula  
Pain: you did not know this trap existed.  
Relief: here is how to protect yourself.  
Control: now you are smarter than the system.  
Trust: follow for survival clarity.

Each post must move readers through all four emotions within 20 seconds.
