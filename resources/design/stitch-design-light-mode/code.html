Yes, the basic compliance checkup is completely free. We provide a summary of your potential tax obligations. If you need a detailed written report with specific action items and legal citations, we offer a paid upgrade.

Our scoring engine is based on the latest Portuguese tax laws (2025 updates) and reviewed by certified accountants. However, it is for informational purposes only and does not constitute official legal advice.
