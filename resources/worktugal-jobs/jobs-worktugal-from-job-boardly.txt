How this works
What kind of jobs should be posted here?
Remote friendly roles that are open to EU based professionals. This works best for mid to senior roles where clarity and fit matter.

Is this a recruitment service?
No. We are not recruiters and we do not source candidates. We provide a curated space for relevant roles to be seen by the right audience.

Do you guarantee applications or hires?
No. We do not guarantee outcomes. We focus on relevance and clarity so teams avoid unqualified volume.

Who sees my job?
Your job is visible to professionals interested in remote work in Europe. Some roles may also be highlighted through our owned channels, based on relevance.

What does “verified” mean?
We review listings for clarity, relevance, and alignment with remote hiring in Europe. This helps keep the board focused and credible.

Can I edit my job after posting?
Yes. You receive an edit link after posting and can update the role at any time.

Do I need to include a salary range?
We strongly recommend it. Roles with clear compensation tend to receive more relevant interest.

Is this suitable for agencies or recruiters?
We prioritize direct hiring teams. Agencies are reviewed case by case.

What is the Custom Hiring Setup for?
It is for teams posting multiple roles or needing help defining requirements before publishing. It is not candidate sourcing.

Is this only for Portugal or Lisbon?
No. The focus is Europe wide. Roles must be compatible with EU based professionals.

Can candidates apply directly through the site?
You can contact us before posting. We are happy to clarify whether your role is a good fit.

Is this the right place if I want maximum reach or mass promotion?
Probably not. This is a focused board built for relevance and fit. If your goal is high volume exposure or mass promotion, other platforms may be a better match.
