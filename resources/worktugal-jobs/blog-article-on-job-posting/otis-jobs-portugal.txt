About Otis:

Founded in 1853, Otis is a pioneer in safe and reliable elevator technology. Headquartered in Farmington, Connecticut, USA, Otis operates in over 200 countries and territories, employing approximately 70,000 people worldwide. The company maintains millions of elevators and escalators globally.

Otis is known for its world-renowned brands, including Otis elevators and its line of escalator and moving walkway products. The company's commitment to innovation and safety ensures its products are used in some of the world's most iconic buildings, such as the Empire State Building in New York City and the Burj Khalifa in Dubai.
