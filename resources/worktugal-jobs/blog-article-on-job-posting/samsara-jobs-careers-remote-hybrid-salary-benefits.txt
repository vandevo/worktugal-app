What Makes Samsara a Great Place to Work?
Beyond the high-paying jobs, Samsara offers a fantastic work environment and benefits package designed to support its employees’ well-being and professional growth. Here’s a glimpse at what you can expect:

Flexible Work Arrangements: Samsara offers a variety of work arrangements, including remote, hybrid, and on-site options, allowing you to choose the setup that best suits your lifestyle.
Competitive Salaries: Samsara’s salaries are highly competitive, with roles ranging from Customer Service Representatives earning around $49,754/year to Directors of Sales earning up to $492,286/year.
Generous Benefits: Samsara provides comprehensive benefits including:
Healthcare: Best-in-class health, dental, and vision insurance.
Fertility Support: Up to $15,000 annually in fertility and adoption reimbursement through Carrot.
Wellness: Samsara’s Hootfund provides quarterly reimbursements for wellness, family care, and professional development.
401(k) Plan: Employer matching up to 4% of your contributions.
Equity Incentives: All employees participate in equity grants, allowing them to share in Samsara’s success.
Flexible PTO: Encouraging a healthy work-life balance.
Mental Health Support: Samsara partners with Modern Health to provide mental wellness benefits.
Paid Parental Leave: Expert support through Cleo for expecting parents and through your baby’s first birthday.
Sabbaticals: Paid sabbaticals are offered to eligible employees after 5 years of service.
