About the Multinational

Ageas is one of the leading international insurance companies with a long history. Founded in 1824, its headquarters are in Brussels, Belgium. Ageas is present in 13 countries in Europe and Asia, employing around 45,000 people worldwide. It is known for its solid financial foundation and its ability to offer customized solutions to its clients.

With over 200 years of history, Ageas continues to grow and expand its global presence while staying true to its values of trust, care, and professionalism. The Belgian multinational operates in various insurance market segments, including life, health, and non-life, always focusing on customer satisfaction and continuous innovation.
