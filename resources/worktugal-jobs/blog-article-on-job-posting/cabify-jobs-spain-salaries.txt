Cabify's Hiring Spree in Spain: Salaries Exceed €4,000 a Month 🚗
Spanish mobility giant Cabify is on a recruitment drive, offering competitive salaries and a chance to work in Europe.
Van Vo
Aug 19, 2024
Spanish ride-hailing company Cabify is expanding its operations in Spain, creating numerous job opportunities with salaries exceeding €4,000 a month. The company, already a leading mobility platform in Spain and several other countries, is seeking professionals across various departments, from technology and operations to marketing.




With headquarters in Madrid, Cabify boasts a global workforce of over 1,500 employees and continues to expand its operations year after year.

About Cabify

Founded in 2011, Cabify has grown into a global mobility platform connecting users with drivers and businesses, offering transportation solutions in various cities around the world. With headquarters in Madrid, Cabify boasts a global workforce of over 1,500 employees and continues to expand its operations year after year.

Beyond its ride-hailing services, the company provides solutions for businesses and has recently ventured into logistics.
