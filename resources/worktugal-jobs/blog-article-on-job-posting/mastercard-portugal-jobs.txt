About Mastercard: A Legacy of Innovation
Founded in 1966, Mastercard has become a leading force in the global payments industry. With headquarters in Purchase, New York, the company operates in over 200 countries and territories, employing over 24,000 individuals worldwide.

Mastercard is known for its innovative solutions, including digital payment technologies, security features, and data analytics services. The company is committed to fostering a more inclusive digital economy, and in addition to its primary brand, owns other recognized brands like Cirrus and Maestro.
