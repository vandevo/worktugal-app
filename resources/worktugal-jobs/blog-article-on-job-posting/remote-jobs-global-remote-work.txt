Remote: A Global Employment Pioneer
Remote emerged in 2019 with a simple yet powerful mission: to open up the vast potential of the world for every person, business, and country. Their vision is a global marketplace where anyone can work from anywhere, fostering a more connected and equitable economy.

The company itself embodies the spirit of remote work. Remote’s team members hail from over 75 nationalities and speak more than 50 languages, reflecting their commitment to diversity and inclusivity. They have no physical offices, believing that employees are most productive and engaged when they have the freedom to work from their preferred location.

Departments at Remote
Remote is organized into a diverse set of departments, all working toward the shared goal of making global employment seamless and accessible. Let’s explore some of these key departments:

Sales and Sales Enablement
The Sales team is responsible for bringing new customers on board, helping employers tap into the global talent pool. Sales Enablement provides resources, training, and materials to keep the sales team well-equipped and informed.

Legal
The Legal team ensures that Remote’s global operations comply with local and international labor laws and regulations.

International Expansion
This team is on the front lines of Remote’s mission, responsible for establishing and managing Remote’s legal presence in various countries.

People
The People team plays a vital role in fostering a positive and inclusive work environment, overseeing areas like total rewards, employee development, and recruitment.

Product
The Product team is constantly iterating and improving the Remote platform to make it easier for businesses to manage their global teams.

Remote Financial Services
This team ensures that Remote’s financial transactions are secure and compliant with regulations.

Operations
The Operations team provides exceptional support to customers throughout the employee lifecycle, from onboarding to payroll to benefits.

Design
The Design team is responsible for crafting a user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing experience for Remote customers.

Engineering
The Engineering team is the backbone of Remote’s technology, building and maintaining the platform that powers their global employment solutions.

Finance
The Finance team manages the company’s financial health, ensuring that Remote can continue to grow and expand its services.

FinTech
The FinTech team helps Remote stay ahead of the curve in payments and financial services, enabling a smooth and efficient experience for customers.

Marketing
The Marketing team champions Remote’s story, sharing the message of how their platform can empower businesses to hire talent from anywhere in the world.
