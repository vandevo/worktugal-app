Benefits and Perks for Remote Employees
Cover Genius understands the importance of a positive work-life balance for its employees. Here are some of the exceptional benefits and perks they offer:

Healthcare Benefits: US employees enjoy comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance.
Paid Parental Leave: Cover Genius provides paid family leave for all parents, offering support during important life milestones.
Retirement Benefits: Employees have access to a generous 401(k) with company matching, along with regional retirement plans to secure their financial future.
Flexible Working Hours: Recognizing the importance of individual needs, Cover Genius embraces an outcome-focused approach to work, allowing for flexible schedules.
Employee Stock Options: Cover Genius rewards its employees with ownership in the company, allowing them to share in the success they help create.
Giving Back Globally: Through their social impact arm, CG Gives, employees contribute to microfinance projects in impoverished countries, fostering social entrepreneurship and promoting equality.
