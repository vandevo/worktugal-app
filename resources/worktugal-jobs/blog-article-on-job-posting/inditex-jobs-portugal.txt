Inditex: A Global Fashion Leader
Inditex, founded in 1985, has grown into one of the world's largest fashion retailers. Headquartered in Arteixo, Galicia, Spain, the company is renowned for its innovative business model that seamlessly integrates design, manufacturing, distribution, and direct sales to consumers. This model allows for rapid adaptation to changing market trends, ensuring they stay ahead of the curve.

The company boasts a diverse portfolio of brands, including:

With a presence in over 215 markets, including 97 with physical stores, Inditex operates a vast network of over 6,800 stores globally. They are a significant employer in the fashion industry, employing over 165,000 individuals worldwide.
