About Feu Vert
Feu Vert is a French company founded in 1972. The company has solidified its position as one of the leading automotive maintenance networks in Europe. Headquartered in Écully, a city near Lyon, the company is expanding its operations to various countries, with over 450 service centers currently spread across several European countries.

Feu Vert employs over 6,000 workers and offers a wide range of services, from vehicle maintenance and repair to the sale of automotive parts and accessories. The company operates under different brands to adapt to the needs of customers in each market where it operates. In addition to France, Feu Vert has a presence in countries like Spain, Portugal, and Italy, strengthening its position as a leader in the automotive service segment.
