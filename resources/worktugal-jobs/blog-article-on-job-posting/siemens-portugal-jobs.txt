About Siemens
Founded in 1847, Siemens is a global technology leader headquartered in Munich. The company operates in over 190 countries, employing more than 320,000 professionals worldwide.

Siemens is active in digital industry with automation and digitization solutions, smart infrastructure developing technologies for buildings and smart energy grids, mobility, and healthcare.

In Portugal, Siemens has been present for nearly 120 years, with over 4,000 employees. The company has played a key role in developing national infrastructure, offering solutions in energy, industry, mobility, and building technologies. Siemens has multiple locations, including the Lisbon Tech Hub, the Cranes Engineering Hub, and the expansion of its factory in Corroios.
