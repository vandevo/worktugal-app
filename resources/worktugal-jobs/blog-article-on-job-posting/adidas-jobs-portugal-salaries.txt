About Adidas:

Established in 1949 in Herzogenaurach, Germany, Adidas remains a global leader in the sportswear industry. The company operates in over 160 countries with over 60,000 employees worldwide, solidifying its global presence and impact.

Beyond the Adidas brand, the multinational also owns other renowned brands like Reebok and TaylorMade, expanding its product range and reaching diverse audiences.
