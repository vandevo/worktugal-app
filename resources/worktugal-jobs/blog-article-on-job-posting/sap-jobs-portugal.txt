SAP: A Global Leader in Business Software
SAP SE is a global tech giant specializing in enterprise resource planning (ERP) software. Founded in 1972, SAP has grown to become a leading provider of business solutions, helping companies across various industries manage their operations efficiently.

With a presence in over 180 countries and a workforce of over 111,000 employees, SAP offers a unique opportunity to work for a global company with a strong commitment to innovation.

Compensation at SAP Portugal
Salaries at SAP Portugal vary based on the specific role and experience level. Here’s a glimpse into the salary ranges for different positions, organized from highest to lowest:

Other Software and Application Analysts and Programmers: €3,600 - €3,800 per month (gross average). This role typically offers one of the highest salaries due to its specialized nature and responsibilities.

Product Manager: €3,000 - €3,200 per month (gross average). Product managers are compensated based on their strategic responsibilities in managing products.

Software Programmer: €2,175 - €2,375 per month (gross average). This role encompasses a broad salary range, influenced by experience and qualifications.

SAP Consultant: €1,380 per month (average). Salaries range from €740 per month for recent graduates to over €3,000 per month for senior consultants with extensive experience.

Remember, these salary figures are estimates and may vary depending on individual factors. For more accurate and up-to-date information, consider consulting platforms like Glassdoor or reaching out directly to SAP Portugal.
