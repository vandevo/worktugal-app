    Businesses, banks, governments, and currencies only work because people *believe* they will work. That trust fuels transactions, investment, hiring, and growth.
    
3. **Supply chains and labor**
    
    Goods and services still need to be made, moved, and delivered. That means physical infrastructure and real human (or now AI-assisted) labor.
    
4. **Capital flows**
    
    Money moves through banks, loans, investments, crypto, stocks, and real estate. The economy runs because capital is always chasing returns.
    
5. **Technology and automation**
    
    AI, software, and platforms now reduce friction and speed up all of the above. But tech doesn’t run the economy, it **amplifies** whoever already has demand, trust, labor, and capital.
    

For you in Lisbon, building Worktugal, the layer you’re operating in is **local trust and demand.** You’re plugging into all five forces by:

- Creating **IRL events** (demand and trust)
- Selling **visibility to partners** (capital flow)
- Using **AI and automations** (tech/efficiency)
- Building a **platform with community gravity** (social infrastructure)
