## Market Gaps

1. **No Self-Service Compliance Tools Exist**  
   Every competitor relies on humans for advice or service execution. No platform allows freelancers or remote professionals to instantly test whether they are compliant with Portuguese Finanças, Segurança Social, or AIMA rules. There is no digital “checkup” product.

2. **Overpriced and Manual for Basic Tasks**  
   Tasks like activity registration, VAT regime checks, or social security declarations are sold for €200–€1000. These are simple form-filling and logic tasks that can be automated through Supabase + Stripe + Make.com workflows with email templates and decision logic.

3. **Language, UX, and Accessibility Gaps**  
   Most competitors’ sites and tools are in Portuguese, designed for locals, and confusing to non-natives. They lack English clarity, step-by-step UX, and emotional transparency. There’s no easy onboarding or mobile-first experience.

4. **No AI-Driven or Self-Updating Systems**  
   Every compliance or accounting service is static. None update dynamically when VAT thresholds, fiscal rep deadlines, or AIMA document lists change. Your approach (Perplexity sweeps + AIRR protocol) guarantees always-fresh legal context without manual rewriting.

5. **Community Channels Unused**  
   None of these competitors leverage Reddit, LinkedIn, or Telegram as live intelligence sources. These are the platforms where pain signals originate (“Got a Finanças letter,” “NISS missing,” “My accountant ghosted me”). You can mine, respond, and redirect those users instantly.
