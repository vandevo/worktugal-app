1. Vision & Goal

Mission: Build Portugal’s first automated self‑assessment tool that lets freelancers and expats instantly see whether they’re compliant with Portuguese tax, social-security and immigration rules, and then routes them to the right professional (accountant or tax lawyer) if they aren’t.

Value: Most freelancers don’t know if they need to register for VAT, whether NISS is required for their residence renewal, or if they must appoint a fiscal representative. They fear fines but hate bureaucracy. The checkup turns their confusion into clarity and gives us a high‑quality lead funnel.

Distinction: We are not giving legal advice; we’re offering a structured self‑check anchored on official rules (Finanças, Segurança Social, AIMA). For actual filings or legal opinions, we hand off to certified professionals.

3. Minimum Viable Question Set (v1.0)

Structure the form into five steps and collect email before showing results:

Identity & Residency – name, email, nationality, visa type, NIF (yes/no), NISS (yes/no), lease registered (yes/no), fiscal representative (yes/no).

Activity & Earnings – whether activity is open, CAE/CIRS code, start date, estimated turnover, VAT regime (Article 53 vs. normal).

Tax & Social Security – IRS filing status (previous year filed?), any Finanças letters/fines, quarterly SS declarations up to date?

Urgency & Language – how urgent the case is, preferred language, a brief description of the main worry.

Consent & Result – explicit consent to store data and route to a certified professional; then show a score and send a personalised summary via email.

Each question should be phrased as a self‑check, not advice. Example: “Have you earned more than €15 000 this year?” instead of “Do you need to register for VAT?”

4. Scoring & Routing Logic

Green (Compliant): Activity open, NIF/NISS present, turnover below threshold, declarations up to date, no Finanças letters.

Yellow (Needs Setup): Activity missing or turnover approaching €15 000, VAT not chosen, NISS or fiscal rep missing but no urgent deadlines.

Red (Urgent): Missing NISS/NIF when required, activity opened late, fines/letters received, turnover already above €15 000, Social Security declarations missed.

Green submissions get a thank‑you email and optional educational content. Yellow submissions are invited to connect with an OCC‑certified accountant. Red submissions are routed to a tax lawyer or specialist.
