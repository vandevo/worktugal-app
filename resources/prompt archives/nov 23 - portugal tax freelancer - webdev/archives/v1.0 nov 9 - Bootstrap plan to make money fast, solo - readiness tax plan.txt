Bootstrap plan to make money fast, solo

1. Narrow the product

Start with one lane, Portugal freelancer basics.
Use your existing 3-minute Compliance Checkup as the entry, then sell a paid Readiness Report and optional human review.

Free, Compliance Checkup. Captures email and key facts, returns a score and a to-do list.
Paid, Readiness Report, €39, PDF plus dashboard, covers NIF, Abertura de Atividade, IVA threshold, quarterly IRS payments, Segurança Social start, receipts rules, penalties and deadlines.
Paid add-on, Accountant Review, €79 to €149, asynchronous review of the Readiness Report and documents, 48 to 72 hour turnaround, you keep a margin.

2. Keep the stack tiny

Frontend, Bolt.new React app at app.worktugal.com.
Backend, Supabase for Auth and Postgres, RLS on user_id.
Payments, Stripe Checkout, one time for the report, separate product for the review.
Automation, Make.com to write Stripe events into Supabase, generate a PDF, send SES email, open an Airtable row when a review is purchased.
Intel, Parallel Find All for source harvesting, not for advice, Perplexity for citation checks, store citations with each rule.

3. Ship only what proves value

MVP screens, Checkup, Paywall, Receipt, Report page, Review upload, Status page.
PDF template, 4 pages, summary, score, exact next steps, penalties and due dates, citations.
Schema, users, reports, report_items, citations, orders, reviews.

Minimal SQL,
reports, id, user_id, score, risk_level, created_at.
report_items, id, report_id, code, title, body, severity, citation_id.
citations, id, url, source_type, retrieved_at, snippet.
orders, id, user_id, stripe_id, product, amount, status.
reviews, id, report_id, status, assignee, notes.

4. Price and offer

Readiness Report, €39, includes one update within 30 days.
Accountant Review, €79 early bird, rises to €149 when you have two accountants.
Bundle, €99, report plus review, limited to first 50 buyers.

5. Acquisition this month

LinkedIn and Reddit, publish a plain English checklist, link to free Checkup.
Telegram group, post the penalty table excerpt and a link to the Checkup.
Partner page, add two visible review slots per week, visible scarcity.
Lead magnet, “Portugal Freelancer Starter Kit”, email gated, feeds FluentCRM.

6. Proof and retention

Turn the Report into a living page, let users mark tasks done, send one nudge per week.
Ask for a one-line testimonial after review, show initials and city.
Add an upsell later, Quarterly VAT Reminder, €19 per quarter, email plus checklist.

7. Guardrails

Never claim legal advice, always show citations and dates.
Hard code all numeric rules in your edge functions, no AI math.
Show a freshness badge per rule, green within 6 months, amber older, red unknown.

8. Ten day build plan

Day 1 to 2, schema, Supabase tables, Stripe products, Make webhooks.
Day 3 to 4, Checkup form and score logic, paywall, receipt page.
Day 5, PDF generator, SES email, Report page with citations.
Day 6, review intake, file upload, Airtable queue, status page.
Day 7, seed twenty rules with citations, run tests on three real cases.
Day 8, landing copy and screenshots, FluentCRM welcome, two emails.
Day 9, LinkedIn and Reddit posts, Telegram announcement, partner page.
Day 10, ship bundle offer, collect first five paid reviews, publish two testimonials.

9. Metrics to watch

Checkup to paid report conversion, target 8 to 12 percent.
Report to review conversion, target 25 to 40 percent.
Refunds under 5 percent.
Time per review under 30 minutes.

Operator Take
Sell the Readiness Report now, bolt on human review, keep AI as research only.
