Accounting Desk Google Trends Audit (Portugal)
=====================================================

Scope: NIF, IVA, IRS — worldwide and bilingual (EN + PT)
Source: Google Trends 2004–2025
Goal: Identify timing, intent, and monetizable entry points for English-speaking foreigners operating in Portugal.

-----------------------------------------------------
1. Macro Behavior Map

Keyword | Core Intent | Trend Direction | Spike Timing | Conversion Opportunity
---------|--------------|-----------------|---------------|-------------------------
NIF Portugal | Entry (setup ID) | Steady rise since 2016 | Jan–Mar, Sept | Get your NIF fast services, automation setup
IVA Portugal | Compliance (monthly/quarterly VAT) | Sharp rise since 2023 | Apr–Jun, Oct | Simplify VAT filings, Automate invoicing
IRS Portugal | Panic (annual income declaration) | Seasonal + sharp 2025 spike | Apr–Jun | Tax filing in English, Avoid fines

-----------------------------------------------------
2. Language Split

Segment | Language | Search Terms | Audience Type | Pain Level | Buy Potential
----------|-----------|---------------|----------------|-------------|---------------
Locals | Portuguese | NIF Portugal, IVA Portugal, IRS 2025 Portugal | Residents, freelancers | Moderate | Low–medium
Foreigners | English | Portugal tax number, Portugal VAT, Portugal income tax for expats | Relocators, remote workers, digital nomads | High | Very high

Insight: The volume looks Portuguese, but the growth comes from English-speaking relocators since 2021.

10. Final Summary

- The search demand is structurally foreign-driven since 2021.
- Spikes = opportunity windows; troughs = setup and education windows.
- NIF = entry. IRS = panic. IVA = retention.
- English-speaking users are 3–5x more likely to convert than locals.
- The winning play is predicting seasonal confusion and automating rescue funnels before they hit.
