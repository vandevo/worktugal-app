Classification Rules

Proceed when
• All communication is written and timestamped.
• Payment is reversible or in escrow.
• The path fits a known safe template (Renewal Pack, Partner Pass, Paid Call).

Delay when
• Proof is partial.
• Bureaucracy needs one more document.
• Energy cost > expected return today.

Exit when
• Counterparty is evasive or disrespectful.
• Contract has no English version.
• Trap risk ≥ 1 and revenue impact = 0.

Scoring (0–2 each)
Revenue impact, Energy cost (inverted), Trap risk.
Proceed ≥ 3 with Trap = 0.
Delay 2–3 or Trap = 1.
Exit Trap ≥ 2.
