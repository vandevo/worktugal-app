Research lane
Portugal tax and compliance. Special tax regimes. Foreign residents.

Objective
Map and compare the former Non Habitual Resident regime (NHR) and the current IFICI regime in Portugal, with the goal of explaining who qualifies, who is excluded, what income types benefit, and how the change affects freelancers, remote workers, employees, and foreign founders. This research will be used to publish a Worktugal decision firewall article that prevents irreversible tax mistakes.

Scope and profiles
Focus on
• Former NHR beneficiaries
• New foreign tax residents after NHR closure
• Freelancers under simplified regime
• Employees
• Remote workers
• Digital nomads
• Foreign founders and consultants

Time window
NHR rules from 2018 to closure
IFICI rules from introduction to confirmed 2026 applicability
No speculative future reforms
