-------------

Most people thought I was crazy to walk away.

Then COVID hit. The Gulf got unstable. The "safe" choice had a price nobody talks about.

I moved to Lisbon with nothing figured out.

First I built RatherThis — sustainable e-commerce, bamboo toothbrushes, planting trees. It taught me how to build something from zero.

Then I noticed a bigger gap. Everyone moving to Portugal was making the same expensive mistakes. NISS missing. Lease unregistered. Tax residency miscalculated.

The same people asking about Portuguese bureaucracy started asking something else. "Where do I find real AI jobs in Europe?"

Not the LinkedIn noise. The roles at companies actually building this stuff.

So I built a jobs board. 545+ hand-curated AI roles across Anthropic, GitLab, Databricks, Mistral AI, Stripe, and Figma. Real salaries. Real applications. Updated daily.

The Portugal stuff is still there. It's just not the headline anymore.

Current: "I left Emirates for Lisbon. Now I write about what it actually costs to stay."

New (Option A): "545+ AI jobs across Europe. Founder @ Worktugal. Ex-Emirates, now in Lisbon."

- States what you do now (AI jobs). Not what you used to do.
