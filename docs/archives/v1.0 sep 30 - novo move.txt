Who is the Portugal D7 Visa for?
The D7 Visa is designed for non-EU/EEA nationals who want to live in Portugal and can support themselves with passive
income, like pensions, investments, or rental income. It provides a 2-year residence permit, which can be extended for
an additional 3 years. After five years, you can apply for permanent residency or citizenship. To qualify, you‚Äôll need to
show stable income, secure accommodation, and meet a few other basic requirements.
